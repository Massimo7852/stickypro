Sticky Pro — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-05-16 · Last updated: 2026-05-16 · Version 1.5

Short version: Sticky Pro stores your notes locally on your Mac and (optionally) syncs them through your own Apple iCloud account. We do not run any server, do not collect analytics, do not track you, and do not see your notes.

1. What Sticky Pro is

Sticky Pro is a sticky notes application for macOS. It is published by Massimo Del Pizzo (Italy). Contact: massimodelpizzo@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

None directly. Sticky Pro does not communicate with any server operated by the publisher. All data created in the app stays on your Mac unless you choose to enable optional features described below.

3. Where your notes are stored

  • Local storage (always): notes metadata in UserDefaults; audio/photo/video files in ~/Library/Application Support/StickyPro/ on your Mac.
  • iCloud sync (optional, controlled by macOS): if you are signed in to iCloud and iCloud Drive is enabled, notes metadata is synced via Apple's NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore and media files via your private iCloud Documents container iCloud.com.massimo.StickyPro. Data flows through Apple's iCloud — we never see it. iCloud is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

4. Optional features that involve third parties

4.1 AI actions (Anthropic Claude)

If you provide your own Anthropic API key in Settings, the AI actions (⌘J) send the selected text or note content to Anthropic's servers for processing. We don't relay or store this data. Your API key stays on your Mac. Anthropic's data handling is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy. This feature is disabled by default.

4.2 Local network sharing

The "Send to LAN" feature (✈ button) uses Apple's MultipeerConnectivity framework to find other Macs running Sticky Pro on the same Wi-Fi network and send a copy of the note. Communication is end-to-end encrypted (TLS-like) and stays inside your local network. No internet involved.

5. On-device processing

  • Speech-to-text (dictation): processed offline by Apple's Speech framework. Audio never leaves your Mac.
  • OCR on pasted images: processed offline by Apple's Vision framework. Images never leave your Mac.
  • Encryption (private notes): AES-GCM 256-bit with a key stored in macOS Keychain, protected by Touch ID. The key never leaves your Mac. Biometric data is handled by Apple, never accessed by us.

6. Permissions Sticky Pro asks for

  • Microphone: only when you start a voice note (🎤) or dictation (💬).
  • Camera: only when you take a photo (📷) or record a video (🎥).
  • Speech Recognition: for dictation and audio note transcription.
  • Local Network: for LAN sharing.
  • Notifications: for snooze/reminders.

You can revoke any of these in System Settings → Privacy & Security at any time.

7. No tracking, no analytics

Sticky Pro does not include any analytics SDK, crash reporter, advertising identifier, or telemetry. We do not know who uses the app or how.

8. Children's privacy

Sticky Pro is not directed to children under 13. It collects no personal data.

9. Data retention and deletion

You can delete any note at any time. Deleting the app from your Mac removes all local data. iCloud-synced data is removed via your Apple ID account; see Apple's documentation for managing iCloud data.

10. Changes to this policy

Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact

For privacy questions write to massimodelpizzo@gmail.com.
